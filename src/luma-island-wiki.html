Travel to Luma Island to go on an epic adventure. Go solo or play with up to 4 friends and family members, this island is yours to discover!

Traverse the island solving quests and helping the locals through their struggles. Dangerous spider caves, ancient temples, mind-bending puzzles and hidden treasures await you!

Begin your extraordinary journey in a humble old caravan and rise to the grand achievement of building a majestic manor that you can proudly call your home.

Pick from seven unique in-depth professions each with its own progression path. Upgrade your tools to gather rare materials and collaborate with your teammates to become the masters of your crafts.

Development

Luma Island is being developed by Feel Free Games, a small indie studio founded in 2021 by veterans from the 3D industry. The game represents their debut title, showcasing their passion for creating engaging and family-friendly gaming experiences.

The development team, though geographically dispersed across the globe, maintains a unified vision of creating a cozy yet adventurous multiplayer experience that brings friends and families together.

Key Development Features:

  • Built with modern game development technologies
  • Focus on cross-platform multiplayer experience
  • Regular community feedback integration
  • Continuous updates and improvements planned

Gameplay

Core Features

  • Single-player and multiplayer modes supporting up to 4 players
  • Seven unique in-depth professions with progression paths
  • Housing system from caravan to majestic manor
  • Quest system with local NPCs
  • Various locations including spider caves and ancient temples
  • Mind-bending puzzles and hidden treasures

Progression System

Players begin their journey with a humble caravan and gradually work their way up to building a grand manor. Along the way, they can:

  • Master different professions
  • Upgrade tools and equipment
  • Collect rare materials
  • Collaborate with other players

World Exploration

The island features diverse locations for players to explore:

  • Dangerous spider caves
  • Ancient temples with mysteries
  • Hidden treasure locations
  • Various quest-giving NPCs

Feel Free Games(Developer & Publisher)

Founded by veterans from the 3D industry in 2021, Feel Free Games is a small Netherlands-based indie developer.

Although Feel Free Games' very diverse team members are scattered across the globe, they are united by a passion for gaming - and for making games.

Luma Island is their first game.
